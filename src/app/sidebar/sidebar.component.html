The size of storms

The Saffir-Simpson hurricane scale is used to describe the strength of hurricanes based on wind speed, which helps emercency managers estimate the threat of a storm. Category 1 storms have winds over 74 mph, while category 5 storms have winds over 155mph.

 About the data

Conditions of air temperature, wind, and sea surface temperature are set to average September values 1979-2011.
